Ingredients:
 - 1 cup warm water
 - 1 teaspoon sugar
 - 1 package active-yeast
 - 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
 - 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
 - 1 teaspon salt
 - Dash or two of Italian Spices

Directions:
 1. Mix water, yeast and sugar in a bowl to proof. Let stand 5 minutes.
 
 2. Add olive oil, salt, spices and flour, mix until dough forms a ball. Let rise for     30 minutes.
 
 3. Divide in half (2 thin crusts) or roll out all dough (1 thick crust). Place on     oiled pizza sheet and add toppings. 

 3. Bake 12 to 17 minutes or until the crust looks done and toppings crisped to perfection.
